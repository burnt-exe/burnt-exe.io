Chess Campaign Plan

Skunkworks Projects Initiative

1. Storytelling Approach

Goal: Create a compelling narrative that captures the hearts of potential sponsors, donors, and media.

Steps:

  • Share the Boys' Journey: Write a concise but impactful story about the boys, detailing how they came together, their passion for chess, and the challenges they've faced.
  • Visual Impact: Collect visual media to humanize the story. Use the group photo and capture videos of the boys playing chess or talking about what chess means to them.
  • Personal Testimonies: Gather written or video testimonies from the boys and their families on how chess has positively impacted their lives.
Learn More

2. Sponsorship and Fundraising Ideas

Goal: Raise funds and secure corporate sponsorships for chess equipment, jackets, and future opportunities such as competitions or educational support.

Steps:

  • Corporate Sponsorships: Reach out to local Johannesburg businesses and companies focused on youth development or education for sponsorship.
  • Crowdfunding Campaign: Set up a GoFundMe or BackaBuddy campaign to raise funds for chess equipment, coaching, and personal development.
  • Chess Tournaments for Charity: Host a chess tournament in Johannesburg to raise funds. Participants would pay an entry fee.
  • Mentorship Programs: Partner with local organizations to create mentorship and career guidance programs for the boys.
Get Involved

3. Engagement and Media Outreach

Goal: Increase awareness and create engagement with the local community, sponsors, and media to amplify the campaign's impact.

Steps:

  • Partnership with Local NGOs: Contact the Sci-Bono Discovery Center to propose a partnership for educational outreach or sponsorship.
  • Social Media Campaign: Create a hashtag and digital campaign to spread awareness. Use visuals and personal stories to encourage people to share and donate.
  • Media Features: Reach out to local radio stations, newspapers, and blogs to feature the story of Emmanuel and the boys.
